Ideation using Padlet

11/02/2021

As part of our ideation process, we utilised Padlet to brainstorm a series of different health issues.

Though we had a few ideas we knew we wanted to explore further, brainstorming as many issues that we could think of would keep our horizons open.

After we established common issues, we then expanded on each issue with a few different methods for how one might track said issue.

For example, if one were to choose Diet as the main issue to bring forward and develop, one may wish to track fluid intake, calories consumed, blood pressure and so forth.
