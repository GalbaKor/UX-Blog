As part of our ideation process, we utilised Padlet to brainstorm a series of different health issues.

Elevator Pitches

18/02/2021

Pitch One - Sleep

For students with sleeping troubles who would like to regain control of their health via better sleep.

Simply Sleep is a mobile app that provides tools for tracking the amount one sleeps, what times they sleep and related lifestyle features such as caffeine consumption and daily mood.

Unlike other sleep based apps (such as Sleep Monitor) that only target the sleeping cycle, our product would incorporate the afore mentioned related issues.

this guides users and allows them to discover how activities throughout the day can influence sleep.

Pitch Two - Exercise

I'd like to pitch to you Shape Up.

Shape Up is a mobile app targeted at busy adults in the 25-35 range who live sedentary lives and don't think they have time for exercise.

It will offer small incremental activities that can be worked into your day without major disruption to your daily routines.

Unlike competing apps such as Sworkit, setup will not be involved and overwhelming to a beginner and will allow for short bursts of activities throughout the day such as 1 to 15 minute workouts, desk exercises and timed challenges such as 'how many calf raises can you do before the kettle boils'.

Persona Creation

As part of our research process, we interviewed each other and compiled the results to create a persona that we felt encompassed our issues, personality and desires.

Our persona is a 20 year old student who is a gamer and finds falling asleep challenging. they struggle with mental health issues and have been negatively affected by covid 19.

User Journey Map

04/03/2021

We took the data from our persona and constructed a user journey map for our app.
The app works best with long term use and so there is a slow but steady positive increase in feelings throughout the stages.
The idea is that the user inputs their habits (caffeine intake, exercise amount, scree-time usage) each day.
They then note their mood when they wake up and the app automatically tracks their sleep amount and quality.
Combining the data creates a picture through which the user can interact and form their perfect routine.
